Baburaoji Gholap College, Pune was established in 1989 and is managed by Pune District Education Associations. The college is affiliated to University of Pune. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate Degree programs in Arts, Science and Commerce in various streams. The aim of the college is to create the right atmosphere that will enable students to realize their potentials and to increase the knowledge. It also provide affordable education to the students.
The college has a placement cell that provides proper guidance to the students that helps them in their placements. It regularly organizes training sessions on soft skill development, career planning classes,.
Accredited by
National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) with ‘B’ Grade
