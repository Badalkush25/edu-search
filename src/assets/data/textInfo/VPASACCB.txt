Vidya Prathisthan & Arts Commerce and Science College is one of the institutions established by the Vidya Prathisthan. The institute was established in the year 1994 and is re- accredited with grade “A” by NAAC. It is affiliated to University of Pune and all its programs are approved by the AICTE, New Delhi. The college offers with various under graduate and post graduate courses in field of science, arts and commerce. It has state of art infrastructure and provides with latest and modern facilities at its campus for faculty and students.
