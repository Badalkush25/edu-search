A private co-educational English Medium School established in 2004. The School is automatically associated with uncompromising discipline, consistency in academic excellence and above all, the dedication of the Management & Staff.
The school is recognized by the Government of Maharashtra but is financially unaided. The School is owned and managed by the Vidya Pratishthan, Baramati, a Charitable Trust with the objective of catering to the educational needs of students hailing from rural areas and providing economically backward students with freeships, free hostel facilities, books etc. The School has the facilities required by a standard educational Institution, for the physical, mental and moral growth and development of its students. It has a large Campus of about eight acres and a School Building having spacious and modern Classrooms, Multipurpose Hall, Laboratories for Science and Computers, Library, Activity Rooms etc."
