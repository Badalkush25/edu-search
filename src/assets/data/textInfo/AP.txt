The Laxmanrao Apte Junior College was established in the year 1976.
Admission  to the college are through the centralized admission system. The cut off percentage for std. XI science stream is highest in Pune, constantly for the past 23 years.
The Junior college that provides education to students of std.11th and 12th, offers education in the following streams :
Science with Electronics – started in 1983.
Science with Computer science – started in 1987.
Science with Information Technology – started in 2001.
Subjects for Pure Science Students are :-
English, Physics, Chemistry, Maths, Biology and Information Technology or German, EVS and Physical Education.

Subjects for Biofocal Students are:
English,  Physics, Chemistry, Maths, and Electronics or computer science, EVS and Physical Education.
