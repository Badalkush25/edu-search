"Rosary School is a co-educational school that educates students from nursery to class XII. Affiliated to the Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations, New Delhi, School aims to develop sensitivity towards the environment among its students.
 School provide state-of-the-art infrastructure for its students. The campuses have well-ventilated classrooms, spacious courtyards for indoor games, and facilities for outdoor activities. Apart from the basic necessities which are kept clean and hygienic at all times, there are computer labs, Science labs and Audio-visual rooms and resource centres."
