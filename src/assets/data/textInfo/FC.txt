Fergusson College is a prestigious autonomous institution located in Pune, Maharashtra. It was established in the year 1885 and provides many courses in the science and arts fields.
It is affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University and functions under the University of Pune. The college has two wings, Junior Wing which includes graduating school students appearing for the Higher Secondary State Certificate examination.
The Senior Wing offers master’s and bachelor’s degree programmes in disciplines such as life sciences, social sciences, creative sciences, humanities, languages, computer sciences, and electronics. Vocational and doctoral programmes are also offered here.
It has received an 'A' grade by NAAC or the National Assessment and Accreditation Council and has consistently ranked within the top 10 arts and science colleges in India.
