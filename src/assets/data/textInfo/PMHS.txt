"Progressive Education Society is the renowned institution established by the Great Visionary Teacher& Educationist Late Shri Shankar Rao Kanitkar Sir in the year 1934. His dream for quality education for students, reaches Modern and Progressive in all the aspects of overall development to harness their skill for the country’s progress.
This legacy has been carried forward under the competent guidance and perseverance of Hon. Dr. Prof. Gajanan R. Ekbote, Chairman, Business Council, P.E. Society.
P. E. S. Modern High School English Medium was sown as a small sapling in the year 1993 with the motto “Knowledge is Power” under the bright shade of P.E. Society. This power ignites into wonderful results in the academics, sports, co-curricular activities, environmental awareness and social development"
