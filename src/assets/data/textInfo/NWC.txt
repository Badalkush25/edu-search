Established in 1932, Nowrosjee Wadia College is situated in Pune Maharashtra. Affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU), the college currently offers UG and PG courses in the departments of Arts and Science. At the UG level, the college offers BA in 11 specializations, B.Sc. under 9 specializations and B.Voc. in 2 specializations. At the PG level, Nowrosjee Wadia College offers MA in 4 specializations, M.Sc. in 12 specializations, along with MCA program.
Candidates applying for BA and B.Voc. should have passed 10+2 with 50% marks in any relevant stream from a recognized board.
Applicants who have passed 10+2 with 50% marks in Science stream are considered eligible to apply for B.Sc course.
Admission to BA, B.Sc., and B.Voc. is done on the basis of merit in the qualifying exam/10+2.
