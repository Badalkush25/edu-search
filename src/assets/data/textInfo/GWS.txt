"Greenwoods School is located in Hadapsar, Pune. It is a Other Board school, with a team of 48 dedicated and professional faculties, which are here to ensure that the children get the most from their education.
Greenwoods School was launched in 2009. This school's primary medium of instruction is English and the student teacher ratio is 36:1. The school tries to provide best learning environment.
This school runs classes from 1 to 8 to provide education to students. The current student strength of the school is approximately 1741. It has a library as well with over 1773 books.
The school has given exceptional results in the academic sphere and its students have excelled in extra co-curricular activities too."
