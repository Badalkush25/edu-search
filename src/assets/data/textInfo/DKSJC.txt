Academic Excellence’ is our priority and through rigorous training and evaluations, including periodic tests according to board and CET pattern, we prepare our students for an excellent performance at the HSC board exams and a career in his or her field of interest, be it engineering, medicine, pure sciences or commerce.
Strongly believing in all round development and in building ‘individuals’, KSJC provides students with many opportunities to participate in various co-curricular activities, develop various aspects of his or her personality, acquire the necessary skills and gain self confidence.
KSJC is well equipped with advanced infrastructure including classrooms, laboratories, and a well-stocked library to ensure the best education and placement opportunities for our students.
