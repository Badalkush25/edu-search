The Suryadatta Junior college, another feather in the cap of Suryadatta Group of Institutions, was conceptualized with an aim to provide world class education to the youth of the continuously evolving society that needs to keep pace with the growing world in todays globalized economy. The SJC is approved by Government of Maharashtra and conducts XIth and XIIth Std. as per Maharashtra board curriculum in the streams of Arts, Commerce and Science. SJC has a dynamic and dedicated faculty.
The faculty is totally focused on the all-round development of students. This is supported by the best in class infrastructure, high tech laboratories, an excellent up to date library, a Wi-Fi campus and multiple sports and recreational infrastructure to facilitate a healthy mind in a healthy body.
