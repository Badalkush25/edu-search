"SES GURUKUL is an English medium school affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). We follow the National Council of Educational Research Training (NCERT) curriculum prescribed by it.
SES GURUKUL’s ethos is based firmly on the principles of learning through life and the experiences that shape us. We have designed a curriculum that is flexible and ensures ‘learning to learn’, thus focusing on developing the child as an active learner.
Core Values :
Discipline that comes from within, with a sense of freedom and commitment to personal and social responsibility.
Integrity and truthfulness in all transactions.
Team-work and a spirit of mutual trust and cooperation.
Love and Respect for self and others."
