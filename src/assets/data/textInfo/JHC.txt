Jai Hind High School and Junior College is an educational institute in Pimpri, a town in Pune, India established in year 1949.[1] It is located opposite to the Jijamata Hospital, on the Deluxe Road. The institution comes under the Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal Corporation.[2] It is a government institute and comes under the administration of the Zilla Parishad. The institute acts as a center for Secondary School Certificate(SSC), Higher Secondary Certificate(HSC), and other equivalent examinations. The school has 4500 students enrolled in 5th grade to 12th grade.[3]
