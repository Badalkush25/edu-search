A school with a purpose to develop the leadership qualities of gifted students. Affiliated to CBSE, mixed (English and Marathi medium), std V to X : 480 girls and boys. A special curriculum designed to help the students express hidden potentials of their intelligence. An innovative school combining both the Indian culture and modern educational technology. A process to develop the social awareness of students through various projects, Paristhiti Jnana periods, creative expression classes and camps. Special purpose groups to supplement the academic syllabus with practical application of those subjects in the society, with weekly expert guidance.
